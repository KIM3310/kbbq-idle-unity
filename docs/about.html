About KBBQ Idle

KBBQ Idle is a browser-playable WebGL idle game focused on repeatable grill loops, progression balance, and release-safe deployment.

What Makes It Useful

  • Playable session loop with clear buy, grill, flip, and collect flow.
  • Operational status visibility for build loading and deployment checks.
  • Trust pages and support routes that stay visible to players and reviewers.

Ad Placement Policy

  • Gameplay area stays primary and unobstructed.
  • Sponsored slot is labeled and physically separated from controls.
  • No incentivized clicks or deceptive overlays are used.
